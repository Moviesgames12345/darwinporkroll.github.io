Our Story

At Darwin Pork Roll, we believe in three simple things: Fresh ingredients, Fast service, and Tasty food that warms the soul.

Located in the heart of Coolalinga, we are a family-owned business passionate about sharing the authentic flavors of Vietnam with our community. Every baguette is baked fresh daily, our broths are simmered slowly for maximum flavor, and our meats are marinated with traditional family recipes.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch on your break or picking up a hearty dinner for the family, we're here to serve you with a smile. Come taste the difference that passion makes!
